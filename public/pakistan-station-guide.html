Major Pakistan station guide

Use this page when you want fast station context before returning to the main schedule tool. For longer routes, the station itself often affects pickup timing, lodging, and onward transit more than the raw train number does.

Lahore Junction

Lahore Junction is the central northern comparison point for many long-distance trains. If you are choosing between Karachi, Rawalpindi, or Quetta-facing routes, it often serves as the easiest timetable anchor because so many named services pass through it.

Karachi City Station

Karachi City is the key southern origin for coastal departures and overnight northbound runs. When you compare fares or duration, it helps to treat Karachi departures as the baseline because the city anchors several of the most searched routes.

Rawalpindi Junction

Rawalpindi matters for travelers balancing northern access with shorter intercity durations. It is especially useful when comparing direct Lahore-Rawalpindi options against longer routes that continue toward Islamabad or Peshawar.
