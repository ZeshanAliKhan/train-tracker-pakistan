Popular Pakistan train routes

The most useful route pages are not random train lists. They should mirror how travelers actually compare corridors: by city pair, duration, overnight fit, and arrival timing.

Karachi to Lahore The core long-distance comparison route with multiple major named services.
Karachi to Islamabad Useful when you need a capital-bound overnight corridor rather than only a Punjab route.
Lahore to Rawalpindi Better for shorter daytime intercity decision-making.
Lahore to Quetta More relevant when western-corridor duration matters as much as schedule fit.
Karachi to Multan A strong south-to-central corridor when full northbound travel is unnecessary.
Karachi to Peshawar A full-length route where day-offset and comfort choices matter most.

How to compare routes quickly

Use the homepage tool to search the city pair first, then sort by the route that best fits your departure window. After that, use the booking and fare pages in this site cluster if the remaining choice comes down to class or budget.
