Rail travel FAQ

This page answers the core questions behind the schedule tool without forcing users to hunt through the homepage sections.

Back to main tool Schedule guide Station guide

Is this a realtime tracker?

No. It is a timetable explorer built around published public schedule data.

Why does the site say “scheduled en route”?

That label means the current time falls inside the train’s published operating window.

What should I confirm before booking?

Always recheck class availability, station notices, and any official service changes before travel.
